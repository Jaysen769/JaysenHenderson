Whispers_of_Heavenly_Death

Whispers of Heavenly Death is a multimedia dance performance based on the poem by Walt Whitman in Leaves of Grass. The animation serves as an indroduction projection, providing context and tone to the performance. Whitmans words are animated echoing his perspective on a human life, and death. In print, text is imbued with natural landscape to accomidate the grandoise nature of the text.
