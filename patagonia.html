100% Traceable Down

Patagonia's 100% traceable down initiative is something that I stand behind. Original Champions of Design studios did a great job with a poster series and a motion piece for Patagonia, this is my own interpretation. In this short animation I traced some drawings from the original posters and forged them into a series of animated .gif files. I then took those files and animated those with aftereffects to create an initially solemn walk through the process that ends on a realization of how these products are brought to us. I found it important to be honest in what it takes to procure the down feathers, but handling the grim situation in a humane way is sobering.
