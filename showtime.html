Showtime "Home Theatre"

A series of television intersticials for Showtime Television network. Focused on the idea of the "home theater" each animation is aimed to resonate with the viewer by relating to the acts done before watching a movie at home. Each piece is concluded by a logo reveal using volumetric light and a series of masks to simulate the glare of the projector.
